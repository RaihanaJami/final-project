About TechMart

Empowering smart shopping through technology.

TechMart is an innovative online tech store created to bring the latest gadgets and electronics to users with a seamless, modern shopping experience. Our goal is to make technology shopping fast, easy, and accessible for everyone.

This website was designed and developed as a final project for the Web Design class, demonstrating key front-end web development skills such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and responsive design.

TechMart showcases essential features like user login, product catalog, shopping cart, form validation, and theme switching, all built from scratch without any backend framework.

Dedicated to building simple, powerful web experiences.

Vision: To make technology shopping accessible to every student, professional, and enthusiast.
Mission: Combining design and functionality to deliver a fast, user-friendly experience.
